You stumble through the door, met only with a few cursory glances from a tired-looking couple fidgeting by a counter, and a woman sitting behind it. The couple catches sight of your hand and flinch in unison, while the woman- presumably, the owner- doesn't seem surprised in the slightest. "I'll be with you in a minute, love," she drones, her tone indicating she didn't think nearly as high of you as her words indicated. "Just don't drip all over my floor." She turns back towards the couple, and the three of them return to whatever they were doing before- the couple searching their pockets and bags, and the woman staring blankly into space. You...

Step into a large room containing a large, round table and myriad of chairs to the right of the entrance. Might as well find somewhere to sit while you wait.

Turn around. You don't feel like dealing with whatever was going on here.
