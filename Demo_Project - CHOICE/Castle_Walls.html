Castle Walls

You arrive just inside the castle's first set of walls, finding yourself in a much more impressive town square. An exquisite fountain jets water into the air, a beautiful crocodile hewn from stone sitting prettily in the center, a twisted imitation of a grin plastered on its extremely detailed face.

Approach the fountain.

That garrison of knights holding swords seems approachable enough!

Turn back and bodily haul yourself into the moat. For whatever reason.
