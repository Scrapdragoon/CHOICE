Training Quarters

Two knights in armor black as ink approach you as you draw near the training garrison. "Halt, friend," the left one lilts, his singsong voice a far cry from the harsh growl you expected. "Halt, friend," the right one attempts to lilt, her vocal chords clearly not built for such things. "I wouldn't come any closer if I were you. Our knights are training, and their swords are quite sharp." "It would be unwise for someone like you to get in their way," the left one croons, the tone in his voice suddenly gaining a condescending tinge. You can almost hear the smirk curl onto his lips as he continues. "You're lucky we stopped you... I doubt they would have even noticed you!" A smarmy, impish giggle echoes throughout his armor.

You shove your dripping hand in their faces.

Whatever, you don't have time for this. You turn around.
