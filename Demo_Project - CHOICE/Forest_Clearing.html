Forest

You wake up in a clearing, with no memory as to who you are, how you got here, or how exactly you ended up with a dagger impaled though your right hand. It appears to have been bleeding for some time, but to you, it's almost as if it wasn't there- you can't feel it in the slightest. You decide, intelligently, not to touch it. You (carefully) push yourself to your feet, (gingerly) brush off your backside, and take a look at your surroundings. A dirt road leads off into the distance to your right, and the maw of an ominous-looking cave sits directly behind you. All you want is to get this dagger out of your hand before the pain assumedly returns. What do you do?

Approach the cave.

Follow the dirt road, like a sane person.
