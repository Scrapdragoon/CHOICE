Small Town

You arrive in a small town. A cursory glance around you reveals several buildings- an inn, a tavern, and a few humble homes. Then, you notice the giant castle looming to the north. Was that always there? Its magnificant drawbridge lies flat across a deep, murky moat. Where to?
